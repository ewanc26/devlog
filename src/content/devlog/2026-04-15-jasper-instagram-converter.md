## @ewanc26/jasper

An Instagram to Grain/Spark converter with full OAuth flow. Features alt text overrides for photo imports, daily chunking and resumable imports for large exports, rate-limited publishing, and a web interface at jasper.croft.click. OAuth scopes include gallery and gallery item permissions.
