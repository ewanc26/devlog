## Supporters

Integrated `@ewanc26/supporters` for Ko-fi webhook handling and GitHub Sponsors events. The website's `/webhook` endpoint accepts Ko-fi POSTs, validates the token, and appends events to the PDS.

## Popfeed

Added a Popfeed recent reviews card to the homepage.

## Donations

Added Monero (XMR), Bitcoin (BTC), and Ethereum (ETH) donation addresses to the footer. The footer was modularised into separate components.
