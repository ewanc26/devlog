## socialsync

A Minecraft mod that syncs player statistics, advancements, and play sessions to AT Protocol records. The server tracks play sessions; the client integrates AT Protocol OAuth with PKCE, DPoP, and PAR. Features a 4-category sync consent system, encrypted session storage (AES-256-GCM), and a ModMenu config screen. Branded as "Social Sync" v0.5.0.
