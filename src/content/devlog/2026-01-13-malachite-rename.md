## malachite

Renamed from `lastfm-importer` to malachite (a greenish-blue copper mineral — sits in that teal/green range). Added Spotify import support alongside Last.fm, interactive mode with credential auto-load, input validation, dynamic CSV parsing, and a redesigned interactive menu. Improved rate limiting with proactive dynamic batching and server-learned limits.
