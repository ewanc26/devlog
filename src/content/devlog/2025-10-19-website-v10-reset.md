## website v10

Tenth iteration of ewancroft.uk. Nuked the entire codebase and started fresh — the commit message was just "RESET" in capital letters. Previous versions had accumulated architectural debt from incremental changes; starting clean meant a proper component structure, consistent styling, and no legacy workarounds. Built with SvelteKit, Tailwind CSS, and TypeScript. The pattern of rebuilding rather than refactoring continues — each iteration is cleaner than the last, even if the frequency is slightly embarrassing.
