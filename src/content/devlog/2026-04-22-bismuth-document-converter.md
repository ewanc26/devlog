## @ewanc26/bismuth

A converter that takes `site.standard.document` records from AT Protocol and renders them to Markdown. The `DocumentRenderer` supports math (KaTeX), code syntax highlighting, and proper facet handling. The CLI can fetch documents directly from a PDS and output formatted Markdown.
