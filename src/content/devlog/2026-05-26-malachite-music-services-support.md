### New Service Imports
Major update to Malachite, adding support for importing listening history from **Apple Music** and **YouTube Music**. This significantly expands the utility of the tool for users moving between streaming platforms.

### Branding & Fixes
- **Branding:** Updated branding and service references across the web interface.
- **Jasper Import:** Fixed an issue where Jasper imports would fail if the `posts_1.json` files were located in different subdirectories within the ZIP archive.
- **Version Bump:** Bumped `malachite-web` to v0.4.4.
