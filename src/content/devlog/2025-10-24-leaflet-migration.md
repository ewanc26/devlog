## blog platform migration

Started migrating blog content from WhiteWind to Leaflet. WhiteWind had stagnated; Leaflet offered a more active and feature-rich platform on the AT Protocol. Used the WhiteWind-to-Leaflet converter built earlier to handle the format transformation. The migration required converting all existing posts, restructuring content to match Leaflet's schema, and setting up the new publication. Not a quick process — backporting the full WhiteWind archive took time.
