## happy mac easter egg

Added a hidden Easter egg to the website. Clicking the version number in the footer exactly 24 times triggers a pixelated Happy Mac icon bouncing across the viewport from left to right, accompanied by a recreation of the classic Macintosh startup chord. The animation takes about 15 seconds, then disappears. The click counter resets and it can be triggered again. The number 24 references the original Macintosh announcement on 24 January 1984.
