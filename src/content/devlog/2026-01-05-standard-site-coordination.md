## standard.site adoption

Adopted standard.site lexicons across projects. The key insight: individual features matter less than coordination in a decentralised ecosystem. If everyone builds their own lexicons for the same concept (blog posts, links, profiles), nothing interoperates. Standard.site provides shared schemas for common content types, meaning records published by one tool can be read by any other. This is the actual promise of AT Protocol — not just decentralisation, but interoperability. Migrated the blog, devlog, and other publications to standard.site lexicons.
