## jollywhoppers membership

Officially joined Jollywhoppers. Started with atproto-connect, a Fabric Minecraft mod that connects Minecraft to the AT Protocol — achievements post to Bluesky, bridging the game world and the social network. Hosted on Tangled. The mod caught the group's attention and led to joining. Now works on other Jollywhoppers projects (not Witchsky itself, despite the wolf-themed client concept). The collaboration model fits: small projects, shared infrastructure, people building what they find interesting.
