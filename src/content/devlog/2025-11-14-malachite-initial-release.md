## malachite

Started as `lastfm-importer` — a CLI tool for importing Last.fm scrobbles to AT Protocol as Teal.fm records. Features TID-based record keys, adaptive rate limiting, resumable imports, deduplication, dynamic CSV parsing, and interactive mode with credential auto-load. Later renamed to malachite (a greenish-blue copper mineral) and gained Spotify import support.
