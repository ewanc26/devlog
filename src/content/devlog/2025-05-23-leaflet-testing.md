## leaflet evaluation

Evaluated Leaflet as a replacement for WhiteWind (which had stagnated). Leaflet has a more playful and fleshed-out feel, but the AT Protocol integration was very early. Key gripes: publishing requires a dedicated subdomain (doesn't work with the existing website setup), and the workflow was inconvenient compared to WhiteWind's simpler model. Decided to wait and see how the platform developed before committing to a migration.
