## witchsky concept

Contemplated building a wolf-themed Bluesky client, inspired by what dame.is accomplished with anisota.net for moths. The concept: dark theme, moon phase integration in the UI, lycanthropic visual identity, and a generally more atmospheric experience than the default client. Never committed to building it — the scope would spiral into maintenance headaches and feature creep. Later joined Jollywhoppers, which works on other Bluesky client projects, making this concept largely theoretical.
