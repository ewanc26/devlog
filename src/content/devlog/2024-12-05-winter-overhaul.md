### The Overhaul
I've completed a full overhaul of the website. This wasn't just a redesign, but a fundamental rethink of the site's architecture and purpose.

- **Aesthetic:** Moved to a much cleaner, more minimal design that prioritizes readability and content discovery.
- **Simplification:** Removed many redundant features and simplified the navigation.
- **Identity:** Added the start year (2023) to the copyright notice to reflect the project's longevity.
- **Privacy:** Re-added a clear privacy statement and updated the error page to be more helpful and less jarring.
- **Mobile:** Made significant improvements to mobile visibility, including hiding certain complex elements (like full datetimes) on smaller screens to reduce clutter.
